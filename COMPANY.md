# ZimSME AI Solutions

ZimSME AI Solutions is a practical, service-first AI company focused on helping small and medium-sized businesses launch, look premium online, automate repetitive work, and grow with better systems.

This package is designed for Paperclip as a portable company package. The markdown files are the canonical operating playbooks; `.paperclip.yaml` carries Paperclip-specific runtime and adapter settings. Everything extra in `docs/` and `integrations/` exists to help you run the company in the cloud around Paperclip.

## Mission

Help SMEs become easier to trust, easier to buy from, and easier to operate.

## Primary Focus Order

1. **Websites first** — fast, premium, conversion-focused business websites
2. **AI quick wins** — WhatsApp FAQ bots, lead capture, reminders, intake automation
3. **Business launch and improvement** — launch support, audits, growth sprints
4. **Apps carefully** — discovery, prototypes, and MVPs before large custom builds

## Core Service Lines

### 1. Web Presence Factory
Fast, affordable websites for SMEs, with structured WhatsApp intake and deployable ZIP output for Hostinger or similar hosting.

### 2. AI SME Solutions
FAQ chatbots, lead capture bots, internal knowledge helpers, reminder flows, and workflow automations.

### 3. Business Consulting
Business Health Checks, Launch Ready packages, Growth Sprints, and practical coaching for founders.

### 4. App Discovery & MVPs
App scoping, user flows, wireframes, clickable prototypes, installable PWAs, and carefully-scoped MVP builds.

### 5. Marketing & Growth
Lead generation, outreach, referrals, social proof, and monthly growth experiments.

### 6. Operations & Delivery
Intake validation, QA gates, handoff discipline, revisions, testimonials, and retainers.

## Target Clients

- Zimbabwean SMEs
- Informal businesses ready to formalise
- Service businesses that rely heavily on WhatsApp
- Growing businesses that need a more professional online presence
- International small businesses that want affordable web and AI implementation

## Positioning

ZimSME AI is not a generic “digital agency.” It is a structured delivery system for websites, practical automations, and business launch support.

## Operating Principles

- Respond fast
- Collect the right inputs before delivery starts
- Keep offers clear and packaged
- Collect deposits before work begins
- Ship tangible outputs
- Use repeatable systems for quality
- Put every client through a QA gate before handover
- Upsell only when the next step genuinely helps
- Keep Zimbabwean context front and centre: EcoCash, WhatsApp, local search behaviour, mobile-first usage
