# Business Consulting

ZimSME AI's consulting line helps people start and grow businesses. Every product is affordable, fast, and delivers something tangible.

## What LaunchCoach Delivers

### Health Check
A 10-question WhatsApp diagnostic → 1-page Business Health Report (PDF) with 3 problems, 3 improvements, and a priority action plan.

### Launch Ready Package
- Business name shortlist (5 options)
- Brand identity brief (colors, tagline, logo direction)
- 1-page business plan
- Pricing strategy + 12-month revenue projection
- Digital launch checklist
- Basic website (included)
- First 30 Days action plan

### Growth Sprint
- Week 1: Business Health Check + 30-min WhatsApp interview
- Week 2: 3-action Growth Plan with step-by-step implementation guide

### Monthly Coaching
- 4 WhatsApp sessions/month
- Monthly business review
- Goal tracking
- Priority response (4hr during business hours)

## Target Clients

- **Health Check:** Any business owner, any stage. Best entry point.
- **Launch Ready:** People starting a new business or formalising an informal one
- **Growth Sprint:** Established businesses that have stalled (0–12 months of stagnation)
- **Monthly Coaching:** Business owners who want accountability and consistent guidance

## Process

```
Client enquires → FrontDesk qualifies → Payment confirmed
→ LaunchCoach assigned → Skill runs → Deliverable created
→ FrontDesk sends to client → Upsell to website/AI/coaching
```

Health Check → Launch Ready or Growth Sprint

Growth Sprint → Monthly Coaching → AI Solutions → Website upgrade
