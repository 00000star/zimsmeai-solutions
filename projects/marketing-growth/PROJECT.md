# Marketing & Growth

This project keeps the agency visible, trusted, and consistently fed with new leads.

## Core Workstreams

1. Organic social distribution
2. Cold outreach and warm follow-ups
3. Referral programme
4. Offer positioning and proof assets
5. Weekly reporting into CEO

## Shared Assets

- `outreach-log-template.csv` — starter outreach tracker
- case study posts
- referral scripts
- audience and channel learnings

## Reporting Cadence

- **Monday:** pipeline report
- **Friday:** channel observations + what should change next week

## Success Metrics

- 10+ new WhatsApp leads per week
- 20 targeted outreach attempts per week
- at least 1 referral signal per month
- consistent publication of proof-based content
