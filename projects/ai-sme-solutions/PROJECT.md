# AI SME Solutions

This project line turns everyday business friction into practical AI-assisted systems.

## Best Early Offers

- FAQ chatbot starter
- lead capture helper
- enquiry triage flow
- reminder and follow-up automation
- simple internal knowledge helper

## Delivery Rules

1. Map the current manual process first.
2. Build the smallest reliable version first.
3. Document the trigger, action, output, and fallback.
4. Keep credentials out of package exports.
5. Create a QA task before handoff.
