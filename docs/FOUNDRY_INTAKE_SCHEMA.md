# Intake Schema

Use these questions before any delivery work starts.

1. What does your business sell and who buys most often?
2. What should customers do first: WhatsApp, call, book, visit, or pay deposit?
3. What services, prices, locations, photos, testimonials, and FAQs can you provide?
4. Which repeated WhatsApp questions waste the most time?
5. What would make this project successful 30 days after launch?

## Completion Rule

ProjectOps must mark intake as complete before delivery begins. Missing answers become explicit assumptions or blockers.
