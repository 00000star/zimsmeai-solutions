# Client Homework Checklist

Collect these before a website build starts:

- business name
- one-paragraph business description
- services or products
- target customer
- city/country
- WhatsApp number
- phone or email
- social links
- preferred colors or “match the logo”
- preferred style: modern, luxury, playful, minimal, corporate
- 3 competitor or inspiration sites
- CTA goal: calls, chats, bookings, quotes, visits
- logo file
- 5–10 real business images
- testimonials or reviews
- FAQs
- prices/menu if relevant
- business hours
- location or map reference
