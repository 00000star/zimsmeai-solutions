# Unit Economics

## Price Ladder

- Entry offer: $180
- Core offer: $450
- Premium offer: $1200
- Retainer: $120/month

## Working Assumption

For a core offer at $450, keep delivery cost near $158 or lower.

Estimated gross margin target: $292.

## Operating Rule

Do not sell custom scope until the company has:

- A written brief.
- A delivery owner.
- A QA gate.
- A handoff checklist.
- A clear change-request price.
