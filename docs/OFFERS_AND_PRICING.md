# Offers and Starting Price Guidance

These are starting internal guidelines, not hard public promises.

## Websites

- **Starter Site:** USD 180–300
  - 3–5 pages
  - mobile-first
  - WhatsApp CTA
  - deploy-ready ZIP

- **Business Site:** USD 350–700
  - 5–8 pages
  - stronger copy and structure
  - FAQ, testimonials, policy pages

- **Premium SME Site:** USD 900–1800+
  - custom sections
  - more involved architecture
  - stronger content and lead flow design

## AI Solutions

- **AI Quick Win:** USD 250–600 setup
- **AI Ops Package:** USD 700–1500 setup
- **AI Growth Retainer:** USD 80–300/month

## Business Consulting

- **Launch Audit:** USD 50–120
- **Launch Kit:** USD 200–500
- **Growth Sprint:** USD 150–400
- **Monthly Coaching:** priced case by case

## Apps

Sell carefully.

Recommended starting offers:
- discovery workshop
- clickable prototype
- installable PWA
- carefully-scoped MVP

Do not casually promise a large custom app without discovery.
