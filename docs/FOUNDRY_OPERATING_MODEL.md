# Operating Model

## Delivery Pipeline

1. Lead captured
2. Package selected
3. Deposit confirmed
4. Homework complete
5. Delivery brief approved
6. Build
7. QA gate
8. Client review
9. Final handoff
10. Follow-up and upsell

## Agent Org

- **CEO:** Owns strategy, priorities, pricing, and final decisions.
- **Client Relations:** Qualifies leads, collects homework, enforces deposit-before-build, manages handoff.
- **Project Operations:** Turns intake into delivery briefs, stage gates, and owner assignments.
- **Website Delivery Engineer:** Builds deployable static sites from approved briefs and templates.
- **AI Automation Expert:** Builds FAQ bots, lead capture flows, reminder systems, and workflow automations.
- **QA Lead:** Checks mobile, forms, copy, assets, deployment readiness, and client handoff.
- **Growth Lead:** Runs outreach, referrals, proof posts, and weekly pipeline reporting.
